Frequently asked questions
What editions are available?

UnetStack comes in three editions namely Community, Commercial and OEM, each packaging the core components for a different audience, from free academic research to OEM device integration.

Can I use Community in a commercial deployment?

No. The Community edition is for educational and non-commercial use only. To connect to an OEM device you can use the open-source Gateway and UnetSocket libraries; for commercial applications, license the Commercial edition.
